Turnament rules

Scoring system:
  • Solo victory - 3 VP (Victory Points)
  • Union (2 factions) victory - 2 VP each
  • Bene Gesserit Victory by Prediction - 2 VP
  • Solo Fremen Victory by Special Condition - 2 VP
  • Solo Guild Victory by Special Condition - 1 VP
  • Union (2 factions) Victory by Fremen Special Condition - 1 VP each
  • Union (2 factions) Victory by Guild Special Condition - 0.5 VP each
  • Any other possible victory (e.g., Fremen winning at the end of Round 10 when there is no Guild) - 0.5 VP
